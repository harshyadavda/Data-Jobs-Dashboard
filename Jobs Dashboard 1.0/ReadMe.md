## Introduction

The Data Job Dashboard is an interactive Power BI analytics solution designed for job seekers and career planners in the data industry.
It analyzes thousands of job listings to provide actionable insights into the current demand, salary trends, and skill expectations across major data-focused roles such as Data Analyst, Data Scientist, Data Engineer, Business Analyst, Cloud Engineer, and more.

This dashboard enables users to:

- Identify the most in-demand data jobs
- Compare median yearly and hourly salaries across roles
- Explore global job availability
- Track monthly job trends in 2024
- View job platform distribution (e.g., LinkedIn, Indeed, etc.)
- Drill down into any role to analyze:

  - Work-from-home percentage

  - Degree requirement insights

  - Health insurance benefits

  - Job type distribution (full-time, contract, etc.)

Designed with job seekers in mind, the dashboard simplifies job market research and helps professionals make data-driven career decisions‚Äîwhether choosing a specialization, negotiating salary, or planning upskilling.

## Dashboard Overview

The Data Job Dashboard provides a comprehensive and interactive view of the current global job market for data-related roles. The dashboard is structured into two main pages to help users explore high-level insights as well as role-specific details.

### üìÑ Page 1 ‚Äî Data Job Market Summary

![Dashboard Page-1](/Images/Dashboard%20Image-1.png)

This page gives an end-to-end picture of the job market in 2024, allowing users to quickly understand:

- Total job count across all data roles

- Salary star rating based on global compensation trends

- Median yearly & hourly salaries in the industry

- Monthly job trend analysis to observe hiring fluctuations throughout 2024

- Salary comparisons across roles using hourly vs yearly pay

- Top job titles by job count

- Tabular job statistics, including:

  - Job count

  - Median yearly salary

  - Median hourly salary

  - Trend indicator

This page serves as a powerful high-level tool for job seekers to compare roles and identify career opportunities.

### üìÑ Page 2 ‚Äî Job Title Drill-Through

![Dashboard Page 2](/Images/Dashboard%20Image-2.png)

Upon selecting any job title, users are taken to a deep-dive page that focuses solely on the chosen role. Insights include:

- Average yearly and hourly salary gauges

- Percentage of remote (WFH) jobs

- Percentage of postings with no degree requirement

- Percentage of jobs offering health insurance

- Job type breakdown (Full-time vs Contract)

- Global job distribution map

- Hiring platform distribution (LinkedIn, Indeed, ZipRecruiter, etc.)

This page helps users understand the job profile, benefits, accessibility, and geography of each data role, enabling practical career decision-making.

## Conclusion

The Data Job Dashboard gives a clear and interactive view of the current data job market. It helps job seekers understand which roles are most in demand, how salaries compare, and where jobs are available globally. With drill-through insights for each job title, users can easily explore details such as remote work percentage, degree requirements, benefits, and job types.

Overall, this dashboard supports job seekers in making smarter and more confident career decisions based on real data rather than guesswork. It also showcases how Power BI can be used to convert raw job listings into practical career insights.
